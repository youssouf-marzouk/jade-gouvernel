ABOUT

JADE GOUVERNEL

Jade Gouvernel is a New York-based artist, curator, and gallery professional working across fine art, design, exhibitions, and multidisciplinary projects.

Her work explores tension between chaos and control, intimacy and distortion, dreams and the waking world.

  • Exhibiting artist
  • Gallery professional
  • Curatorial project lead
  • The Jaded Gallery
  • Fine Art and Design background
  • Exhibition coordination, installation, visitor engagement, and artist support

Full CV available upon request.
