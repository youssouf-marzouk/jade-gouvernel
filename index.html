ABOUT

JADE GOUVERNEL

Jade Gouvernel is a multidisciplinary artist working across painting, drawing, collage, photography, ceramics, film, and voice, based in Brooklyn, New York.

Born in South Africa to a French father and a Kenyan mother, Jade grew up between continents and cultures. Her work begins in the tension between multiple inheritances: the European and the African, the formally trained artist and the self-taught maker.

At the center of everything she makes is a recurring question: can a person consciously create themselves into existence through ritual, image, repetition, beauty, performance, and devotion?
